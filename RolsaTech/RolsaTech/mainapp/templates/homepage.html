How do I book an installation for a specific product?

To book an installation for your preferred product: Make sure you are logged in, and on your navigation bar, there should be a button "Installations". Click on it, and it will redirect you to your installations page. At the bottom of the visible section, there should be a button "Book installation". Click it, and then you will be redirected to a page where you should be able to select your preferred product for installation.
